How to Play !!

How to get through Friday

The object of the game is to save the neighborhood from the clutches of the nefarious Deebo and his crew. You will battle through 4 bosses of increasing power and difficulty before Deebo appears!

The cycle of attack begins with Craig, then progresses to Smokey, and finally the boss lets loose, with the active player in red.

--- The standard Attack With Fists will do 5 damage, can miss, but is limitless ---
--- Special attacks do 15 damage, never miss, but are limited to 2 per player. Use them wisely ---
--- Having a Drink of Kool-Aid heals the player for 10HP but are limited to 3 per game ---

Can you survive all 5 rounds to protect the Neighborhood?
